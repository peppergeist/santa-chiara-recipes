# Kiwi & Pear Summer Salad

## Ingredients

* 1 cup of cubed pecorino cheese or similar sharp white cheese
* 1 cup of cubed kiwi
* 1 cup of cubed pear
* 1 cup of croutons (plain or garlic flavor)
* 2 tbsp. of olive oil
* 1/4 tsp. of balsamic vinegar

## Preparation

Mix all ingredients together with the olive oil and vinegar. Salt and pepper as preferred. Serves 4.
